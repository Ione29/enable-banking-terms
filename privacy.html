Privacy Policy

This application is a personal, non-commercial tool used solely by its owner for personal financial tracking via a self-hosted instance of Actual Budget.

It is not distributed to, or used by, any other individuals. Account and transaction data retrieved through Enable Banking is used only to sync the owner's own bank account information into their self-hosted budgeting application.

This data is stored only on the owner's self-hosted server infrastructure and is never shared with, sold to, or processed by any third party.
